How It Works

This demo shows a simplified three-step process for decision support.

1. Upload Federal Loan Data

Bring in public federal loan summary files by geography.

2. Combine With Income Data

Join with state-level median income to add local context.

3. Generate DTI Burden

Calculate debt-to-income ratio and assign a simple risk level.

Prototype Notes

This is a visual concept for partner conversations. Data is mock and stored locally in CSV. No login, no personal borrower data, and no production analytics pipeline.
