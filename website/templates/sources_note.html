Data Provenance

Prototype metrics are derived from public datasets and aggregated at the state level.

  1. Federal Student Loan Portfolio by Location (U.S. Department of Education, Office of Federal Student Aid)
    Type: XLS | Update frequency: Quarterly
    URL: https://studentaid.gov/data-center/student
    Citation: Office of Federal Student Aid. (2025). Federal student loan portfolio by location [Data set]. U.S. Department of Education.
  2. American Community Survey 1-year Estimates: Median Household Income (U.S. Census Bureau)
    Type: CSV/XLS | Update frequency: Annually
    URL: https://data.census.gov
    Citation: U.S. Census Bureau. (2025). American Community Survey 1-year estimates: Median household income [Data set].
  3. Consumer Credit Trends: Student Loans (Consumer Financial Protection Bureau)
    Type: CSV | Update frequency: Monthly
    URL: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-credit-trends/student-loans/
    Citation: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. (2025). Consumer credit trends: Student loans [Data set].
