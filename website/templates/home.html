Why BorrowSmart?

Student loan advisors often rely on national averages, but repayment pressure varies widely by state. BorrowSmart demonstrates how state-level debt burden indicators can inform outreach and guidance decisions.

See Method Try Demo

The Core Idea

Problem

National averages hide local affordability differences for borrowers.

Signal

Debt-to-income by state creates a clear burden indicator for planning.

Action

Advisors can prioritize support intensity where burden and trends are highest.
