Help Advisors See Where Student Loan Burden May Be Highest

BorrowSmart is a prototype decision-support tool for nonprofit financial advisors working with borrowers across the United States.

It combines federal loan balances, borrower counts, household income, and origination trends to help teams prioritize outreach, plan counseling capacity, and identify states that may need more support.
